1) Text-to-Speech: Text-to-speech technology is the process of transforming written text, such as words, sentences, or paragraphs, into audible speech. It enables computers and devices to communicate information vocally, allowing users to hear the content instead of reading it.TTS plays a crucial role in accessibility, making digital content available to people with visual impairments or reading difficulties.

Text-to-Speech

2) Speech-to-Text : Speech-to-text technology is the process of converting spoken words and phrases into written text. It involves using algorithms and machine learning to recognize and interpret human speech patterns, converting them into textual form. It enhances accessibility by allowing individuals with hearing impairments to convert spoken language into readable text

Speech-to-Text

3) Video-to-Text : Video-to-Text refers to the process of converting spoken or written content from a video into textual form. T his conversion can be done through various techniques such as transcription, captioning, or speech recognition technologies. It enables videos to be accessible to individuals with hearing impairments, provides search engine optimization benefits, and facilitates content indexing and analysis.
